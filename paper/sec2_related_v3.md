---

Recent LLM-driven analog design work clusters by what the LLM is
asked to do. **Designer cluster:** AnalogCoder [Lai et al., AAAI 2025]
and AnalogCoder-Pro [2025] frame analog design as code generation
from spec; complementary to our work, which assumes a foundry-bound
schematic already exists. **Optimizer / sizer cluster (closest):**
ADO-LLM [Yin et al., ICCAD 2024] couples Bayesian optimization with
LLM-proposed in-context demonstrations. EEsizer [Liu et al., NEWCAS
2025] runs a ReAct closed loop over Ngspice across eight commercial
LLMs. AnaFlow [Ahmadzadeh & Gielen, ICCAD 2025] is a four-agent
explainable workflow on BSIM 45 nm predictive models. LEDRO [Kochar
et al., ICLAD 2025] uses an LLM to shrink TuRBO's search box. Two
concurrent 2025–2026 papers extend the SOTA: AutoSizer [Cheng et al.,
arXiv 2602.02849, 2026] benchmarks reflective multi-agent meta-
optimization across 24 circuits; AnalogSAGE [Wang et al., arXiv
2512.22435, 2025] adds stratified memory and experience replay.
**Benchmark cluster:** AnalogGym [Li et al., ICCAD 2024] is the de-
facto open benchmark suite; our spec evaluator borrows its YAML-
metric idiom but binds it to a closed industrial PDK.

**AnalogAgent [Bao et al., arXiv 2603.23910, 2026]** is the only
prior work that explicitly targets NDA-constrained deployment. They
solve it by restricting inference to tiny on-premise Qwen3 1.7B–14B
open-weight models. Safe-Bridge takes the *complementary* approach:
admit any cloud LLM, prevent PDK content from ever crossing the
trust boundary. The two stories bracket the industrial-deployment
design space.

**Differentiation (Table 1).** EEsizer is the prior system that goes
furthest on raw checkpoint count (eight commercial LLMs), but its
eight are all Western-frontier and it does not develop a cost-
quality Pareto or per-LLM failure taxonomy. AnalogAgent evaluates
four checkpoints but they are all Qwen3-family on-prem tiny models,
not cloud frontier. No prior system evaluates a single China-
frontier cloud LLM, let alone four. Our row is the **only** one
that is ✓ across the conjunction of **multi-LLM evaluation**,
**China-frontier-vendor coverage**, **cost-quality Pareto**,
**industrial PDK**, **per-LLM failure taxonomy**, and **verifiable
NDA-safe substrate** simultaneously.
