\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented an \textbf{eleven-LLM $\times$ seven-metric
spec-contract benchmark for industrial-node analog sizing} on
\textbf{Safe-Bridge}, a verifiable substrate that lets any cloud
LLM drive a real foundry-bound schematic without PDK content
crossing the trust boundary. The eleven checkpoints span seven
vendor families across two countries (6~US + 5~China). The
cost--quality Pareto
(\S\ref{sec:results}) with its reasoning-token premium and the
failure-bucket matrix (\S\ref{sec:failures}) constitute the
reusable contribution for future industrial-EDA LLM evaluations.

\paragraph{Future work.}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Multi-circuit expansion.} OpAmp testbench (the
    \S\ref{sec:intro}/\S\ref{sec:methodology} in-revision hook);
    further targets: comparator, bandgap reference.
  \item \textbf{Multi-PDK / multi-node expansion.} Cross-foundry
    and cross-node generalisation (BSIM-CMG generations, cell
    libraries, process stacks).
  \item Extend \filename{\_normalize\_usage} to capture
    Anthropic / Gemini reasoning-token accounting
    (\S\ref{sec:results-premium}).
\end{itemize}
