## 5.5  Reasoning-token premium — SP3 sharp evidence

A sharp single observation anticipated in this work is the
**reasoning-token premium** on vendors that bill reasoning tokens
in their per-token rate but report them in a separate channel.
Buyers estimating cost from the *visible response text* — and
applying the public per-token rate — systematically under-budget by
a vendor-specific fractional ratio `r/(1−r)`. We define
`r := reasoning_tokens / completion_tokens`, where `completion_tokens`
is the billed completion total surfaced by `_normalize_usage`
and **includes** reasoning. Equivalently, with `R` the hidden
reasoning-token count and `V` the visible-output token count,
`r = R/(R+V)` and the under-budgeting ratio is exactly `R/V`:

- For **Kimi K2.5**, **MiniMax M2.7**, **DeepSeek V4-pro / V4-
  flash**, and **Xiaomi MiMo v2.5-pro** (vendors exposing
  `reasoning_content` and tracked as a separate `reasoning_tokens`
  field by `_normalize_usage` at `src/llm_client.py:21-80`), the
  reasoning fraction is **`r = TBD-from-D6`** (vendor-by-vendor
  breakdown in the appendix).
- For **OpenAI GPT-5.5 / GPT-5.4-mini** the `reasoning_tokens`
  counter is reported as a sub-field of completion tokens; the
  premium is **`r = TBD-from-D6`**.
- For **Anthropic Opus 4.7 / Sonnet 4.6 / Haiku 4.5** and **Google
  Gemini 2.x**: per-iteration reasoning-token accounting is **not
  exposed by the current `_normalize_usage` schema** — the Anthropic
  branch records only input / output / total
  (`src/llm_client.py:42-46`). The Anthropic / Gemini premium is
  therefore reported as **unavailable in v3**; a follow-on schema
  extension to capture `cache_creation_input_tokens` /
  `cache_read_input_tokens` / Gemini `thinking`-block usage is
  flagged in §8 future work.

A practitioner sizing budget against the public per-token rate
(input + output only) for a Kimi-or-MiniMax-style vendor under-
budgets by a fractional ratio of `r/(1−r)`. This is the **labelled
subsection** the introduction promised; we surface it because it is
the single observation most likely to be cited by practitioners
deploying any of these eleven checkpoints in production.
